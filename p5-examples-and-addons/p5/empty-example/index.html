Welcome to the collection game. Use your joystick to move the circle around and collect squares. After 10 rounds, your score will be the amount of time it took you to collect all the squares. Try to get the quickest time!

Press R to change your sprite color to red. Press O to change your sprite color to orange. Press Y to change your sprite color to yellow. Press G to change your sprite color to green. Press B to change your sprite color to blue. Press P to change your sprite color to purple.
